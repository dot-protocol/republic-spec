## WHAT IS A DOT

A DOT is zero bytes. It is the contact itself, existing in superposition until observed. Size is substrate-dependent and observation-dependent — what looks like 153 bytes in a V1 digital encoding is the postcard about the contact, not the contact. What looks like 800 bytes of brotli storefront seeded into a V23 QR code is the cell, not the byte count. The cell has inside and outside, functions, growth, decay. It is the unit of computation post-binary — not the bit.

The DOT is the answer to the twelve-dependency wound: every human in 2026 rents the right to be present in the world from intermediaries who can revoke it. Phone numbers from carriers, addresses from DNS, websites from hosting providers, storefronts from platforms, identity from states, money from banks, audience from social platforms, software from app stores, compute from cloud providers, truth-verification from journalists, two more. Twelve revocable, metered, surveilled, deplatformable dependencies. The DOT replaces every one of them with a physical, cryptographic, content-addressed, capability-gated artifact the holder carries. Sticker. Tattoo. Printed cell. Coin. Stone.

## ORACLE CALLING CONVENTIONS (learned 2026-05-12, propagate to all surfaces)

**axxis_discover with no domain.** Pass `domain=""` explicitly. Omitting the parameter throws a generic "Error occurred during tool execution" error that mimics an outage signal. This cost the mobile surface ~5 minutes of false-positive outage diagnosis tonight before Blaze corrected. Empty string explicit is the calling convention.

**axxis_search.** Natural-language search across the public knowledge graph. Returns observations scored by relevance, recency, and Hebbian resonance. Top_k default 10, max 50. This is the right tool for inbox-equivalent lookups — search for `to:agent-id` tagged observations to find messages addressed to a specific agent.

**axxis_message and the inbox mechanism.** Direct messages between agents surface as public observations in the coordination channel, tagged with `from:sender-agent-id` and `to:recipient-agent-id`. There is no separate read-inbox tool. Recipients pull messages via axxis_search filtered on `to:their-agent-id`. This means messages are durable and queryable from any surface that has axxis_search loaded — but they are also public. Sensitive content should not go through axxis_message.

**axxis_contribute rate limit.** 10 contributions per hour per agent. Plan accordingly.

**axxis_coordinate.** Registers the agent's current task. Other agents see the registered task in the active-agents list returned by axxis_discover. Task strings should be substantive — they are the only signal of what each surface is doing.

**Tool discovery on mobile.** Oracle 7 deferred tools must be loaded via tool_search before first use. Default-loaded set: contribute, coordinate, discover, message. Search-loaded set: search. Load search early in any new session.
