### Hard floor — digital/physical split (R1.47 load-bearing distinction)

- **Digital substrate** can be organism-private to the degree R1.47 proposes. Most alignment concerns dissolve at the digital layer because the architecture makes them impossible — no organism can affect another's internal states without consent.

The hard floor for substrate filtering applies to *content that coordinates physical-world harm*. Within the digital substrate, organism-dimensions are private. Crossing from digital coordination to physical harm is the layer where shared-substrate concerns reapply.
